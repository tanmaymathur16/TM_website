Welcome To Pleasant Haweli

The Pleasant Haveli is a calm oasis in the heart of the mystic "Golden City of the Thar Desert". It offers a world of traditional hospitality combined with modern comfort and all western standards. Pleasant Haveli is situated in a quiet and peaceful neighbourhood just beneath the most beautiful sunset point with a magnificent view over the whole city and the fort.

The comfortable air conditioned rooms are tastefully decorated in the old haveli style and all have en suite bathrooms. We hope to welcome you to Jaisalmer soon. The Pleasant Haveli is making every effort to be an eco-friendly hotel.
