IMPORTANT INFORMATION
Jodhpur is the most convenient airport; it is 285km from Jaisalmer.
Rail:

Rail: there is good connection with India's major cities through Jodhpur. For more information about trains in India please visit Indian Railways' official website at www.indianrail.gov.in and to purchase train tickets online please register at www.irctc.co.in.

Road:

Road: a good network of roads joins Jaisalmer with many destinations in and around Rajasthan. For more information about roads and buses, please visit the Rajasthan Tourism website atrsrtc.rajasthan.gov.in This website also gives lots of information about Festivals and other special events in Rajasthan.
