Our Locations

We are located in the heart of old city of Jaisalmer. The golden fort and havelis (Patwa Haveli, Nathamal Haveli etc), the markets, ATMs (cash machines) and banks, post office, pharmacies, cosmetics stores, garment shops, fruit shops, book shops etc. are all within walking distance of the our hotel. We are located approximately 1.5km from Jaisalmer railway station; the journey takes 10 to 15 minutes by auto rickshaw. From the bus stand to the hotel takes 5 to 10 minutes by auto rickshaw.
